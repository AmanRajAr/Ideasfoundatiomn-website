Workload assessment of ANMs & ASHA at HSC level : At the instance of State Health Society, we has undertaken a workload assessment of Health Sub-centre (HSCs) in Bihar. During the assessment month of the year’2012, the study has been done regarding the functioning of health sub-centre and the workload assessment of ANMs. The primary objective of the study is to assess whether the HSCs have been able to bring health care services within the reach of the people residing in the rural areas and providing relief to the people through other referral hospitals. Within this broad objective, the specific objective regarding workload of ANMs have been identified and assesses in the consultation with Health Sub-centers.During the assessment of workload at HSC level, the sampling of 62 HSCs have been undertaken from 13 blocks of six districts (Nalanda,Gaya, Khagaria, Madhubani, Kishanganj and Begusarai) in Bihar.

About Us

Ideas Foundation has a rich experience in the areas of program design, program implementation and project management. With the help of the multi-disciplinary team, we try to bring out the best design to implement and manage a program efficiently and successfully. “Ideas Foundation is a support institution that is committed to provide quality awareness regarding health issues , capacity building in health issue, Survey & Baseline Studies, Data Collection, Data Validation, Editing, Designing, Curriculum development, Design of training, Creation of training material, manuals, execution of training programs, workshop and seminar. Registered in 2004 as a Non-Government society, has already been established as a pioneering support organization in the field of promotion of Health issue, Innovation in Health Delivery System, Telemedicine, and capacity building of Community based organizations. We aim to meet the needs of the various stakeholders involved in the promotion of Health issues, SHG, Community owned organizations and capacity building of different stake holders. D. Capacity Building on Health Awareness and Preventive Therapy : As implementation agency for World Health Partners ( A BMGF-Project) Bihar project-Current training programme is being continuing at 12 districts of Bihar , i.e. Patna , Vaishali , Muzaffarpur , Gopalganj , East Champaran , West Champaran, Samastipur, Begusarai, Khagaria, Saharsa, Nalanda and Jahanabad districts Training and capacity building for semi urban enterprenuers are being conducted to help them to establish a rural telemedicine center , where a patient can have medical examination form MBBS doctors sitting at medical facility centers at Delhi and Patna. Trainer and a social trainer. Two set of training is being conducted to provide basic skill for rural telemedicine centers and rural health providers for smooth functioning of the project.. E. Capacity Building of Adolescent Girls on Health, Hygiene and Nutrition : We have conducted more than four hundred training programs for the adolescent girls on health, hygiene & Nutrition under the Kishori Shakti Yojana in the district of Patna, Vaishali, Nawada ,Nalanda, Bhojpur , Buxar , Jahanabad & Rohtas in Bihar Each unit under KSY spanned 3-day training program and covered 40 adolescent girls from different Anganwadi Centers. This programme was of Women’s development Corporation(GoB) in association with our partner agency Arunabhashree Society. This programme includes knowledge sharing on Health & Hygiene, Female Reproductive system, Menstruation, Uses and disposal of Sanitary Napkins etc. At the end of the training all participants were given Rs-75/- as travelling allowances, we use to invite local dignitaries to grace the occasion and distribute the amount.
